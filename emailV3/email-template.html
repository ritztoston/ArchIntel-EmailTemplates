Farm Equipment Magazine Features Ranger Crew XP 1000 UTV

Farm Equipment has written an article featuring Polaris' Ranger Crew XP 1000 utility vehicle, which uses an 82hp ProStar 1000 engine, with 2,500 pounds of towing capacity and up to 13 inches of ground clearance. The UTV can haul up to six people, and has a payload capacity of 1,750 pounds, the publication.reported Monday. Read more...
