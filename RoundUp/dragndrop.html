Potomac Officers Club Hosts 2018 Healthcare Summit
Drop Here
  •  
Drop Here
  •   
          On Sept. 19th, GovCon public and private sector representatives gathered for the Potomac Officers Club’s 2018 Healthcare Summit, featuring keynote addresses from Karen Brazell, Department of Veteran Affairs; John Tenaglia, Defense Health Agency, Col. Kevin Seeley, DHA; and Jose Arrieta. The summit also included the Healthcare IT Innovation Panel, led by Dr. Joseph Ronzio, VA Department, and Mark Scrimshire, NewWave Telecom & Technologies, Inc. Suzanne Charleston, LexisNexis Risk Solutions, moderated the event.

          Events like these are possible because of our wonderful sponsors, like Deltek, and audience members from both the private and public sectors. By gathering to hear from the most consequential experts in government and industry, top executives ensure they’re up-to-date on the drivers and technologies fueling our industry. Ours is a unique business with far-reaching consequences for defense, healthcare and IT services, and the company takes its responsibility to foster crucial discussions on these topics seriously.

          At the summit’s beginning, Frazell, the VA’s chief acquisition officer, highlighted two key takeaways: the VA is making modernization a top priority and wants to partner with industry to deliver effective IT solutions to veterans. Expounding on that, she mentioned her desire to bring all stakeholders to the table to leverage best practices and maximize outcomes. Describing her role, Frazell said she’s responsible for the department’s entire acquisition management, which includes contracting, contracting administration, design and construction of new facilities, and facility management. She stressed that the VA, under Secretary Robert Wilkie, is spearheading initiatives to improve veteran customer care across the entire IT enterprise.
