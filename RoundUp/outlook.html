Weekly Roundup | July 9 - July 13, 2018
Executive Mosaic’s Weekly GovCon Round-Up: $400M+ July Contracts & Top 10 Stories

Jim Garrettson, Executive Mosaic CEO
Aerospace-Space: Boeing, Northrop, and Lockheed

          As summer has seen a spate of space-related activity, I’d like to recap some of the major platform companies and players consolidating their satellite portfolios.

          On August 17th, GovConWire reported that Boeing agreed to purchase El Segundo, Calif.-based Millennium Space Systems for an undisclosed sum. The Chicago, Ill., company announced Millenium Space Systems will operate as a subsidiary under the direction of Mark Cherry, vice president and general manager of Boeing’s Phantom Works division. Leanne Caret, president and CEO of Boeing’s defense, space and security business, and a two-time consecutive Wash100 winner said the addition of Millennium Space Systems will help boost the company’s satellite portfolio and meet the needs of a diverse client pool. Boeing expects the acquisition to conclude by the third quarter of this year.
